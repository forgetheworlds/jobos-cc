# Lane Taxonomy — Higher Education Role Families

## 8 Academic Lanes

### Lane 1: academic_admin
**Title Cluster A**: Academic Administration & Program Leadership
- Academic Coordinator, Academic Program Coordinator/Manager
- Academic Affairs Coordinator/Manager
- Academic Operations Manager, Academic Services Coordinator
- Program Coordinator/Manager (Postsecondary, Higher Education, Academic Programs)
- Education Program Manager
- Assistant/Associate Director of Academic Affairs/Programs
- Manager of Academic Programs/Services/Student Success/Student Experience
- First-Year Experience Coordinator/Program Manager
- Student Affairs Coordinator/Manager
- Student Services Coordinator/Manager
- Academic Support Services Manager

**Resume Emphasis**: Program oversight, faculty coordination, student engagement, operational leadership, first-year program scope, institutional alignment

**Cover Letter Angle**: Lead with program management scale (2,000+ students), connect to institutional goals

---

### Lane 2: student_success
**Title Cluster C**: Student Success, Advising, and Academic Support
- Academic Advisor, Senior Academic Advisor
- Student Success Advisor/Specialist
- Educational Advisor, Student Development Advisor
- Student Engagement/Experience Coordinator
- Admissions Advisor
- Graduate/Undergraduate Program Coordinator

**Cover Letter Angle**: Lead with advising outcomes and student success metrics

---

### Lane 3: advising_support
**Subset of Cluster C**: Specialized advising and support roles
- Academic Advisor (specialized populations)
- Student Services roles focused on support delivery
- Roles combining advising with program coordination

**Resume Emphasis**: Direct student support, case management, referral coordination, multicultural advising

**Cover Letter Angle**: Emphasize direct student impact and cross-cultural communication skills

### Lane 7: teaching
**Lower Priority Cluster F**: Teaching Roles
- Lecturer, Session Lecturer
- Adjunct/Contract Instructor

- College/University Instructor

**Resume Emphasis**: Course instruction, student engagement, course design, multicultural education, experiential learning

**Cover Letter Angle**: Lead with teaching across multiple institutions and diverse student populations

---

### Lane 8: reject
Jobs that do not fit any lane or trigger hard exclusions:
- K-12 teaching (unless strong leadership component)
- Corporate training (unless meaningful overlap)
- Sales-heavy education roles
- Technical IT roles (instructional technology without pedagogy)
- Research-track faculty positions (require publication record)
- Roles requiring specific licensure not held (LCSW, RN, etc.)
- Entry-level roles that severely underuse 20+ years experience

**Action**: Skip. Do not generate materials.

---

## Classification Rules

1. **Check exclusions first** — if a job matches any hard exclusion, classify as `reject` immediately
2. **Match by title cluster** — the job title is the primary signal
3. **Verify by description** — the full description should confirm the title signal
4. **Resolve conflicts** — if title suggests one lane but description suggests another, weight the description (actual responsibilities > title wording)
5. **Assign confidence** — how certain are you of this classification? Be honest about ambiguity
6. **Multi-lane jobs** — some jobs span lanes. Pick the primary lane and note secondary alignment in risk_notes
