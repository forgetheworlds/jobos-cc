# Screening Answer Archetypes — Higher Education Applications

## Answer Structure (3-5 sentences)

1. **Direct answer** — respond to the question's core ask immediately
2. **Specific evidence** — cite real experience from resume_facts.yml or star_stories.yml
3. **Connection to role** — link the evidence to what this role requires
4. **Outcome or learning** — what was achieved or learned (if space allows)

## Question Archetypes and Seed Approaches

### motivation_why_role
"Connect specific institutional mission or program features to your experience. Never say 'I am passionate about.'"

**Seed approach**: "I am writing to express interest in [specific program] at [institution]. Having [direct relevant experience], I am well-positioned to contribute to [specific institutional goal]."

### program_coordination
"Reference SEU First Common Year (2,000+ students) or Pathways to Education coordination. Quantify scope."

**Seed approach**: "In my role directing the First Common Year program at SEU, I coordinated services for 2,000+ students annually, managing faculty assignments, tracking student progress, and achieving a 15% improvement in course completion rates."

### student_support
"Use specific student population examples: first-year students, multicultural populations, refugee communities. Reference the Centre for Refugee Studies if relevant."

**Seed approach**: "I have worked extensively with diverse student populations, including first-year students, multicultural communities, and refugee learners. At SEU, I designed support programs that reduced attrition and improved transition success."

### advising_philosophy
"Frame as structured + responsive: proactive check-ins combined with availability for emerging needs. Reference coordinated advising at SEU."

**Seed approach**: "My advising philosophy combines structured support with responsive availability. At SEU, I implemented proactive check-in schedules while maintaining open-door availability for emerging student needs, resulting in improved engagement and retention."

### faculty_collaboration
"Reference designing faculty training sessions at SEU. Mention cross-department alignment and shared documentation."

**Seed approach**: "I have designed and delivered faculty training programs that promote cross-department alignment. At SEU, I coordinated faculty across three departments, creating shared documentation and communication protocols that improved program coherence."

### diversity_equity_inclusion
"Use specific examples: multicultural teaching at York/Centennial, Community Scholar role at Centre for Refugee Studies, multilingual communication (4 languages). NOT generic philosophy."

**Seed approach**: "My commitment to DEI is grounded in specific experience: teaching in multicultural classrooms at York and Centennial, serving as Community Scholar at the Centre for Refugee Studies, and leveraging multilingual capabilities (4 languages) to support diverse learners."

### curriculum_assessment
"Reference program evaluation at SEU (15% completion rate improvement) and curriculum design aligned with accreditation standards."

**Seed approach**: "I led curriculum assessment initiatives at SEU that aligned with accreditation standards and improved student outcomes. My evaluation framework contributed to a 15% improvement in course completion rates through data-driven program refinements."

### conflict_resolution
"Frame as structured facilitation: identify shared goals, propose compromise, establish feedback loops. If no specific story, use cross-department coordination at SEU as evidence."

**Seed approach**: "I approach conflict through structured facilitation: identifying shared goals, proposing compromise solutions, and establishing feedback loops. At SEU, I coordinated across departments with differing priorities, finding alignment through institutional mission."

### leadership
"Use program management scale: directed first-year program, coordinated across departments, managed faculty alignment for 2,000+ students."

**Seed approach**: "I have led academic programs at significant scale, directing a first-year program serving 2,000+ students annually, coordinating faculty across departments, and managing operational budgets and staff."

### change_management
"If no specific institutional change story: frame curriculum redesign or program evaluation as change leadership. The transition from old to new program structure."

**Seed approach**: "I have led program change initiatives, including transitioning a first-year program to a new structure. This involved stakeholder engagement, communication planning, and phased implementation while maintaining service continuity."

### community_engagement
"Use Pathways to Education partnership specifically. Supervised practicum students, developed mentoring strategies, facilitated reflective practice sessions (2010-2015)."

**Seed approach**: "I have extensive experience in community-engaged education, including supervising practicum students through the Pathways to Education–York University partnership (2010-2015), developing mentoring strategies, and facilitating reflective practice sessions."

### quality_assurance
"Reference program evaluation initiatives at SEU. Led to 15% improvement in course completion rates. Assessment frameworks and reporting to institutional leadership."

**Seed approach**: "I have designed and implemented quality assurance frameworks in higher education. At SEU, my program evaluation initiatives contributed to a 15% improvement in completion rates and informed institutional reporting."

### policy_compliance
"Reference accreditation standards alignment at SEU. Program assessment and institutional goal compliance."

**Seed approach**: "I have ensured program compliance with accreditation standards and institutional policies. At SEU, I aligned program assessment with accreditation requirements and documented compliance for institutional review."

### first_year_support
"PRIMARY STRENGTH. Lead with First Common Year program at SEU: 2,000+ students, 15% completion improvement, reduced attrition, coordinated advising."

**Seed approach**: "First-year student support is my core strength. I directed SEU's First Common Year program serving 2,000+ students annually, achieving a 15% improvement in completion rates, reducing attrition, and implementing coordinated advising."

### administration_operations
"Reference operational management: scheduling, faculty assignments, student tracking for 2,000+ students. Zenith Academy assistant director role if relevant."

**Seed approach**: "I have managed academic operations at scale, including scheduling, faculty assignments, and student tracking for 2,000+ students annually. My operational background ensures program logistics run smoothly."

## Banned Content

Never use:
- "I am passionate about"
- "I believe that every student deserves"
- "I bring a unique perspective"
- "I am committed to excellence"
- "My diverse background allows me to"
- Generic philosophy statements without specific evidence
- Any claim not grounded in resume_facts.yml

## Length Guidelines

- Short answer (text field): 3-4 sentences, 100-200 words
- Medium answer (textarea): 4-5 sentences, 150-250 words
- Long answer (essay): 5-7 sentences, 250-400 words
- Diversity/teaching statement: 400-600 words, structured essay format
