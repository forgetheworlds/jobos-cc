# Screening Answer Archetypes — Higher Education Applications

## Answer Structure (3-5 sentences)

1. **Direct answer** — respond to the question's core ask immediately
2. **Specific evidence** — cite real experience from resume_facts.yml or star_stories.yml
3. **Connection to role** — link the evidence to what this role requires
4. **Outcome or learning** — what was achieved or learned (if space allows)

## Question Archetypes and Seed Approaches

### motivation_why_role
"Connect specific institutional mission or program features to your experience. Never say 'I am passionate about.'"

### program_coordination
"Reference SEU First Common Year (2,000+ students) or Pathways to Education coordination. Quantify scope."

### student_support
"Use specific student population examples: first-year students, multicultural populations, refugee communities. Reference the Centre for Refugee Studies if relevant."

### advising_philosophy
"Frame as structured + responsive: proactive check-ins combined with availability for emerging needs. Reference coordinated advising at SEU."

### faculty_collaboration
"Reference designing faculty training sessions at SEU. Mention cross-department alignment and shared documentation."

### diversity_equity_inclusion
"Use specific examples: multicultural teaching at York/Centennial, Community Scholar role at Centre for Refugee Studies, multilingual communication (4 languages). NOT generic philosophy."

### curriculum_assessment
"Reference program evaluation at SEU (15% completion rate improvement) and curriculum design aligned with accreditation standards."

### conflict_resolution
"Frame as structured facilitation: identify shared goals, propose compromise, establish feedback loops. If no specific story, use cross-department coordination at SEU as evidence."

### leadership
"Use program management scale: directed first-year program, coordinated across departments, managed faculty alignment for 2,000+ students."

### change_management
"If no specific institutional change story: frame curriculum redesign or program evaluation as change leadership. The transition from old to new program structure."

### community_engagement
"Use Pathways to Education partnership specifically. Supervised practicum students, developed mentoring strategies, facilitated reflective practice sessions (2010-2015)."

### quality_assurance
"Reference program evaluation initiatives at SEU. Led to 15% improvement in course completion rates. Assessment frameworks and reporting to institutional leadership."

### policy_compliance
"Reference accreditation standards alignment at SEU. Program assessment and institutional goal compliance."

### first_year_support
"PRIMARY STRENGTH. Lead with First Common Year program at SEU: 2,000+ students, 15% completion improvement, reduced attrition, coordinated advising."

### administration_operations
"Reference operational management: scheduling, faculty assignments, student tracking for 2,000+ students. Zenith Academy assistant director role if relevant."

## Banned Content

Never use:
- "I am passionate about"
- "I believe that every student deserves"
- "I bring a unique perspective"
- "I am committed to excellence"
- "My diverse background allows me to"
- Generic philosophy statements without specific evidence
- Any claim not grounded in resume_facts.yml

## Length Guidelines

- Short answer (text field): 3-4 sentences, 100-200 words
- Medium answer (textarea): 4-5 sentences, 150-250 words
- Long answer (essay): 5-7 sentences, 250-400 words
- Diversity/teaching statement: 400-600 words, structured essay format

## Source Attribution

For each drafted answer, note in the output:
- Which resume_facts entry it draws from
- Which star_stories entry (if applicable)
- Whether it was adapted from a seed answer in screening_answers.yml
- Any gaps where the candidate lacks direct experience (so the answer pivots to transferable strengths)
